ABOUT

The TrimGYM was founded 35 years ago. Perhaps that’s why we’re so focused on proper health and fitness benefits, and less on the luxuries that a small percentage of traditional health club members want. Our team has a long and successful history in the gym business. We opened our first health in 1988, building the business to a total of 9 clubs. We floated the company in 1997, and in 2000. We spent time lending our expertise to premium gym, before concentrating on the launch of The TrimGym Group. Our first Gym opened in Chicago in 1988. It was a roaring success, and was swiftly followed by Guildford and Vauxhall. Within the first three years we’d opened 20 Gyms and we've never looked back. We’re ambitious, driven and committed to our members. We’ve received flattering – but we think deserving – accolades from our peers, the press, and most importantly, our members. But we’re not complacent – we’ve got long-term plans for expansion and continued innovation to ensure that we lead the USA budget gym growth. In November 2015, The Gym Group successfully completed thier main goal to opening more than 5+ clubs. In August 2012 The TrimGYM Group was awarded the prestigious Investors in People accreditation on its first attempt. The Gym is the only group operator within the health and fitness sector to be accredited and that shows that how much we really care about and look after our people, providing them with a better working life. In May 2016, The TrimGYM Group claimed another fitness industry first after attaining Gold status from Investors in People, joining the top seven per cent of accredited organisations across the USA. Following an in-depth assessment, the Investors in People report recognised The TrimGym Group as an ‘extraordinary company’ and a ‘great place to work’. It was also praised for making continuous efforts to improve people management as well as taking further strides to reward staff and recognise achievement, such as awarding company shares to all those on the payroll.
